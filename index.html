The Best Hindi Movies According to Navanit

"Let's embark on a journey through the colorful world of Hindi movies with Navanit Sharma's top picks. From old classics to new favorites, these movies are like windows into different worlds, full of fun, emotion, and great stories. Get ready for a cinematic adventure that'll make you laugh, cry, and think!"

My top 3 movies of all time

  • Rab ne bana di jodi

    Directed by Aditya Chopra, this romantic drama stars Shah Rukh Khan and Anushka Sharma. It tells the story of a mismatched couple who find love and happiness through unexpected circumstances. With charming performances and heartfelt moments, it's a feel-good movie that celebrates the magic of love and companionship.

  • Vicky Donor

    Directed by Shoojit Sircar, this comedy-drama stars Ayushmann Khurrana and Yami Gautam. It revolves around the concept of sperm donation and its impact on a young man's life. With witty humor and a fresh storyline, the film explores societal taboos with sensitivity and humor, making it both entertaining and thought-provoking.

  • Barfi

    Directed by Anurag Basu, this romantic comedy-drama stars Ranbir Kapoor, Priyanka Chopra, and Ileana D'Cruz. It follows the story of a mute and deaf man named Barfi and his relationships with two women. With its charming performances, whimsical storytelling, and beautiful cinematography, "Barfi!" is a heartwarming tale of love, friendship, and acceptance.
